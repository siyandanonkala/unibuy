🇿🇦 Welcome to UniBuy

UniBuy is a proudly South African online marketplace built to make buying and selling simple, affordable, and accessible for everyone. Whether you are an individual, entrepreneur, small business, or established company, UniBuy gives you the opportunity to reach customers across South Africa through one trusted platform.

Our Mission

Our mission is to remove barriers to online commerce by providing an easy-to-use marketplace that empowers South Africans to buy, sell, and grow their businesses with confidence.

Our Objectives

  • 🚀 Reduce barriers to entering the online marketplace.
  • 🤝 Connect buyers and sellers across South Africa.
  • 📱 Make online shopping and selling simple and user-friendly.
  • 💚 Provide affordable e-commerce solutions for South Africans.
  • 🏪 Allow businesses to register and create their own online stores.
  • 📈 Support entrepreneurs and small businesses as they grow.
  • 🇿🇦 Promote local businesses and encourage buying local.
  • 🔒 Build a trusted and secure marketplace for everyone.
  • 💡 Continuously improve the platform with innovative features.
  • 🌍 Contribute to economic growth and job creation in South Africa.

Why Choose UniBuy?

  • ✅ Proudly South African
  • ✅ Easy to use on mobile and desktop

  • ✅ Create your own online store in minutes
  • ✅ Buy and sell with confidence

🔒 Privacy & Trust

At UniBuy, your privacy matters. We are committed to protecting your personal information and maintaining your trust.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or personal gain.

Your information is collected only when necessary to create your account, improve our services, facilitate transactions, and help provide a safe marketplace for buyers and sellers.

We believe your information belongs to you. UniBuy is committed to transparency, security, and responsible handling of your data.
