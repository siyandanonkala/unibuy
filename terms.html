📄 Terms & Conditions

UniBuy is a platform for students to buy and sell items with one another. By using UniBuy you agree to:

  • List only items you have the right to sell.
  • Describe items honestly and set fair prices.
  • Arrange payment and handover directly and safely with the other party.
  • Treat other students respectfully in messages and listings.

UniBuy is not a party to any sale and is not responsible for the condition, safety, or legality of items exchanged between users. Trade at your own discretion and meet in safe, public campus locations.
